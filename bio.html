About

Welcome to my About page where you get to learn a little more about me! I am a Professional Writing (PW) major. I came from a town whose high school was called Pewamo-Westphalia (PW). So when my college advisor asked me why I wanted to be a PW major I obviously felt it was meant to be (but not really), let's rewind a bit. Ever since I was little I have loved to read. I begged my mom to teach me to read and I loved reading to my classmates and aunts and uncles. You can imagine my mother was a bit enthused and vexed when I made a long distance phone call to Texas (back when landlines were the most popular form of communication) just to read a story to my aunt. Sometime in early elementary school I started to write my own stories. In sixth grade I had my own short story published, which was exciting. I have a deep desire to write books that takes one on an adventure.

Aside from reading and writing, I enjoy many other things. Things like working (who knew?) and having down time with my friends. I really enjoy my job that I have right now as a student employee at the IPF (Infrastructure Planning & Facilities Building) on campus. If I could work more than 40 hours a week all of the time I probably would. I think I should be concerned that I might turn out to be a workaholic. I am also very sarcastic, so be prepared, and I like to keep people guessing. Maybe the latter will really help me out with my writing.

Now you have seen a bit of my formal and informal side. If you have any questions or want to know more, feel free to e-mail me at my gmail or contact me through my Linkedin account. Thanks for reading!
