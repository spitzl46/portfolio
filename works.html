This is a fictional story that I wrote for a contest. Children's stories that won were put into the Anthology of Short Stories by Young Americans. This story was approved and published in the 2007 edition.
My very first year of college I took an Integrated Arts & Humanities class. One of the main things I learned about my writing from my professor was that I needed to pick one idea, stay focused on that idea.This piece I feel exemplifies the things I learned in her class.
Also my first year of college I took an English class. In that class I learned how to analyse texts, form an opinion on my analysis, and prove my opinion with textual evidence. This is one of my favorite pieces I did for this class.
